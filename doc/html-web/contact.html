Chapter 4. Contact Us

Subscribing to the mailing lists

If you have problems running FreeGuide, you should have a look at the FAQ at first. If that doesn't help, have a look at the already-asked questions on the FreeGuide Users mailing list. If you want contribute actively to the list, subscribe to the list and send a mail to freeguide-tv-users@lists.sourceforge.net.

The developers can be found at freeguide-tv-devel@lists.sourceforge.net and the devel list archives.

Subscribing to the mailing lists

To subscribe to a mailing list, go to the corresponding mailing list manager page where everything is explained:

  • freeguide-tv-users
  • freeguide-tv-beta-testing
  • freeguide-tv-devel
  • freeguide-tv-cvs
