Some vague notes on what to put into the "Grabber Command" box in FreeGuide.

Mainly for my own use (Andy Balaam) but may be useful.

UK done the old (but still post 0.4.3) way with tv_split:

"C:\Program Files\freeguide-tv\xmltv\tv_grab_uk.exe" --config-file "C:\Program Files\freeguide-tv\xmltv\tv_grab_uk" --output "C:\Program Files\freeguide-tv\data\tv-unprocessed.xmltv"
"C:\Program Files\freeguide-tv\xmltv\tv_split.exe" --output "C:\Program Files\freeguide-tv\data\tv-%%Y%%m%%d.xmltv" --day_start_time 00:00 "C:\Program Files\freeguide-tv\data\tv-unprocessed.xmltv"

---

UK the new flashy way - doesn't work since --offset isn't in yet.

"C:\Program Files\freeguide-tv\xmltv\xmltv.exe" tv_grab_uk --config-file "C:\Program Files\freeguide-tv\xmltv\tv_grab_uk" --offset %offset% --days 1 --output "C:\Program Files\freeguide-tv\data\tv-%date%.xmltv"


---

NA the new flashy way:

"C:\Program Files\freeguide-tv\xmltv\xmltv.exe" tv_grab_na --config-file "C:\Program Files\freeguide-tv\xmltv\tv_grab_na" --offset %offset% --days 1 --output "C:\Program Files\freeguide-tv\data\tv-%date%.xmltv"

"C:\Program Files\freeguide-tv\xmltv\xmltv.exe" tv_grab_** --output "C:\Program Files\freeguide-tv\data\tv-unprocessed.xmltv"
