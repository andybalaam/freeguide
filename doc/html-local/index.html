FreeGuide - your TV Guide

FreeGuide is a TV guide program. It allows you to find programmes you are interested in quickly, and ignore the rest.

Download FreeGuide
Version 0.10.9 for Linux, Windows or others

Features

  • Create a personalised TV guide containing only programmes you are interested in.
  • Display only channels you are interested in and create several custom lists.
  • Automatically highlight favourites and have them appear in the guide.
  • Search for programmes by title, sub-title, channel, time etc.
  • Grid or list view depending on your preference.
  • Works for many countries including Argentina, Brazil, Canada, Croatia, Denmark, Estonia, European Union (Various), Finland, France, Hungary, Iceland*, Italy, Israel*, Japan*, Latin America (Various), the Netherlands, Norway, Portugal, Reunion, Romania, Spain, Sweden, Switzerland, the United Kingdom and the United States.
    (* - Linux only)

Note: If you are in North America, during the install you may choose the "United States (alternative - na_dtv)" or "Canada (alternative - na_dtv)" to get free TV listings. Alternatively you can pay for an account with Schedules Direct so you can download listings: Sign Up For Schedules Direct
